Thanks for stopping by my website!

I am a first-year PhD student in the Data Mining Group (DMG) at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Jiawei Han. Before coming to UIUC, I got my master's at Fudan University advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu. I was an undergraduate at ECNU, working with Prof. Yuanbin Wu. My research focuses on Natural Language Processing and Text Mining, especially Text Summarization.
